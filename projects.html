ReVert App

The ReVert mission is to empower people to live vibrantly and elegantly but in a sustainable way. I joined ReVert in June of 2020 to develop an app to accompany a smart powercube. To that end, I developed an app that not only gives customers the ability to control when their plugged-in devices turn on or off, but also provide key data insights into when they should turn their devices on or off based on our analysis of their consumption patterns.

Othello A.I.

Othello is a game I used to play a lot as a kid. When given the opportunity in a final project to choose a game to create an A.I. for, I knew I had to take a crack at Othello. The computer agent uses Monte Carlo Tree Search to quickly find good moves to play, and I have yet to beat it. The game model and A.I. were originally coded in Python and then traspiled to JavaScript for this website using Transcrypt.

Yale Forum

I created Yale Forum along with David Padron-Castillo and Philena Sun during our first year at Yale. The inspiration for the app came from our own experiences feeling a bit lost and searching for answers when we first got to campus. Thus, Yale Forum was born as a way for students to ask niche questions to their fellow classmates and get quick answers.
